About Us

Mintmade Fashion is a clothing line for passionate, young women who loves to mix and match their looks uniquely. Since 2018, Mintmade has been going worldwide.

We understand that time is a valueable asset but limits a modern women's commitment to style. Balancing a career alongside all aspects of our lives is quite tough, without the morning hurdles of deciding what to wear. We realise women everywhere face the same problem--we love to shop but often times find ourselves spending fruitless hours online or in stores.
